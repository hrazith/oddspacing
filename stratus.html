Avance

  • Experience Architecture & UI Engineering
  • James Powers

Redesign of Software and Brand

Avance is a high avalibilty software solution that combines two standard servers into one failsafe system. All data and applications are synchronized between the two servers continuously, in real time, ensuring critical applications are up and running.

My heuristic evaluation shed immediate light on several areas where the usability and UI experience could be improved: The software required a strong and a consistent brand, which would dictate a consistent visual style and information hierarchy across all states of UI interaction. Some areas of the UI failed to impart a clear sense of workflow and navigational clarity, and required redesign of its components. The product also required a sound localization strategy as many of its users were overseas.

BMSS

  • Creative Direction & Interaction Design
  • Gita Dubovis

Branch Manager Supervisory System

BMSS is an tool that allows Control Officers to perform delegated supervision functions and approvals for the branch / complexes. They may also escalate supervisory issues to the Complex when necessary and follow-up on issues identified by complex supervisors.

The system offers several ways to filter and sort through the lists. Users may then save these filters and use it at a later time..

Control officers can escalate trade violations by putting them in context whilst communications.

Comments logged against accounts and trades.

Financial Plan Billing

  • Creative Direction & UX
  • Gloria Petron

A tool to manage billing for fee based Financial Plan engagements.

A view that shows accounts and its document, payment and engagement statuses.

The look up tool

Engagements can be searched by last name or tracking number. If an engagement is not found the system automatically initiates intake process and registers a new enagagement.

The engagement then goes through the approval process where payments methods are setup and all the pertinent documents are collected, verified and previewed.
